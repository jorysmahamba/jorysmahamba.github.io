About Me

I'm a 20-year-old French student preparing to begin my MSc in Mathematical and Theoretical Physics at the University of Oxford this October. My passion lies in discovering the mathematical frameworks underlying the laws of the universe.

I graduated valedictorian from Université Paris-Est Créteil with a dual BSc in Mathematics and Physics, ranking 1st in mathematics and 3rd in physics. My unique academic journey began with finishing high school at 15, followed by three years of intensive Classes Préparatoires (PCSI/PC/PC*) at Lycée Marcelin Berthelot, along with a high-level youth athlete career in handball, karaté and badminton.

Research Vision:

Developing higher categorical structures in non-perturbative quantum field theory, exploring how (∞,1)-categories might provide new mathematical frameworks for understanding quantum gravity and string theory dualities.

Currently interning at LAMA under Dr. Ilaria Mondello on differential geometry, preparing for Oxford while pursuing an independent project on AI applications to string theory. Through my blog "Jorys, The Mathematician", I share accessible insights into mathematics and physics.

Publications & Academic Work

"The Nobel Prize in Physics 2024: A Celebration of Machine Learning's Foundations in Physics"

Survey Article • La Gazette des Mathématiciens, Société Mathématique de France • Under Review

Comprehensive mathematical review of Hopfield Networks and their deep connections to the Ising model, exploring the statistical physics foundations of neural network architectures. Written to celebrate the 2024 Nobel Prize awarded to Hopfield and Hinton.

Statistical Physics Neural Networks Survey

"Exploration mathématique des transitions de phase du modèle d'Ising pour son intérêt dans l'enseignement universitaire de la physique statistique"

BSc Research Report • LAMA, Université Paris XII • June 2024

Rigorous mathematical treatment of phase transitions using measure theory, correlation inequalities (GKS), and thermodynamic limits. Demonstrated absence of phase transitions in 1D versus existence in 2D using Peierls arguments. Presented at UPEC research seminar.

Phase Transitions Mathematical Physics Educational Research

"Machine Learning Framework for Commercial Paper Valuation"

Co-authored Research Paper • BRED Banque Populaire • In Preparation

Developed novel statistical features for ML models in financial valuation, improving prediction accuracy for Commercial Paper pricing in trading environments.

Machine Learning Quantitative Finance
